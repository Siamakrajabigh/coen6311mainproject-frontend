Can I issue more than one service provider?
Currently, you can only book one service provider per service. Our team is working on further development for issuing a single ticket to multiple service providers.

Is this an ethical way to approach for a job?
Yes, the website is an ethical way for approaching new jobs as it maintains transparency between job providers and service providers. The negotiation pay for the users are left to their job providers, hence there is no transfer of personal details like SIN or bank number on this website.
